Greenovation Club Of MCKVIE
- bridging innovation and sustainability.

Introducing the Greenovation Club of MCKVIE! Dive into a world of innovation, as we host exhilarating hackathons and competitions centered on green energy and sustainable development. Proudly sponsored by the SWITCHON foundation and its GBIN program, which empowers West Bengal's college and university students to drive sustainable change through entrepreneurship. Join us in solving the global sustainable development challenges and fostering a new generation of green business leaders, all within the confines of our college campus. Unleash your ecoentrepreneurial spirit with us!
